- Led ISO 27001 certification for Fintual -- passed the external audit on the first
  attempt (April 2025). Coordinated control owners across all squads over ~8 months of
  active work

- Lead a security team of 3 across Chile and Mexico (Security Lead CL, CISO MX). Work
  spans both technical (tooling, infrastructure, incident response) and organizational
  domains (board reporting, regulatory compliance, vendor negotiation, hiring)

- Built and maintained in-house CLI tool that automates identity and access management
  across the company's IdPs, sourcing policies from a GitOps repo

- Built an internal incident management tool (Django) that tracks security incidents.
  Introduced per-incident channels, post-mortem templates, and regulatory reporting to
  ANCi/CMF

- Drove regulatory compliance for CMF and ANCi cybersecurity requirements, including
  board-level reporting

- Rolled out endpoint management (MDM, 125+ devices) and VPN for internal tools across
  the company. Chose tooling that respected user privacy (consent-based remote access,
  split-tunnel VPN) to reduce friction during adoption. Managed network policies as code

- Mentored non-developers in an internal AI upskilling program (~32 participants),
  helping them build working automations for their own workflows, aligned with security
  best practices
